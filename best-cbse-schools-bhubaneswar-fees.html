1. ODM Public School ‚Äî Sishu Vihar, Patia

ODM is probably the most talked-about CBSE school in the entire city right now. Ranked among India's Top 5 schools by multiple surveys and regularly topping Odisha's Class X and XII results, this is the school every parent in the Patia‚ÄìChandrasekharpur belt is angling for.

2. SAI International School ‚Äî Chandrasekharpur

SAI International is the school that put Bhubaneswar on the national school map. Established in 2008, it runs dual affiliation ‚Äî CBSE and Cambridge International (CAIE) ‚Äî which means your child can pursue IGCSE from Class VIII onwards if needed. The 8-acre campus sits right in the Chandrasekharpur IT pocket, making it a logical choice for families in that belt. Separate air-conditioned hostels for boys and girls ‚Äî many outstation families from Koraput, Sambalpur, and Rayagada prefer it for this reason.

3. DAV Public School, Unit-VIII ‚Äî Nayapalli

DAV Unit-VIII is a Bhubaneswar institution in the truest sense ‚Äî established in 1971, with over 50 years of heritage and more than 3,800 students. This is the school where many of today's judges, engineers, and government officials studied, and parents love dropping that fact into every school-choice conversation. The Nayapalli campus has a slower, more community-oriented feel compared to the slicker IT-corridor schools. Teachers tend to stay longer, which parents say matters a great deal.

üìç Location

Unit-VIII, P.O. Nayapalli ‚Äî near the old residential quarters, walkable from the VSS Nagar area. One of the most well-connected school locations in the city ‚Äî accessible from almost any direction without a major highway crossing.

What Parents in Bhubaneswar Actually Say

Talking to parents outside these schools revealed a few patterns that no brochure will tell you:

  • 1
    Commute is the real hidden cost Parents near the Chandrasekharpur schools said the commute is a significant budget item ‚Äî many families spend ‚Çπ2,000‚Äì‚Çπ4,000 a month on school buses from areas like Tamando, Patrapada, or the older Unit colonies. Add that to your annual fee calculation before comparing schools.
  • 2
    DAV teachers stay longer Parents in the Nayapalli area consistently mentioned that DAV teachers tend to remain at the school for many years ‚Äî something they feel contributes to better continuity of learning. At some newer premium schools, high faculty turnover was a quiet but recurring complaint.
  • 3
    The KV surprise A retired CRPF officer standing outside the Pokhariput KV gate told me simply: "Yahan padha ke mere teen bacche alag alag IITs mein hain." (All three of my children who studied here are in different IITs.) The best education isn't always the most expensive one.
  • 4
    ODM entrance is real ODM's baseline entrance exam for Classes I‚ÄìIX and the OSAT for Class XI are not formalities ‚Äî several parents confirmed their children needed one or two attempts. Good academic foundation before applying is advisable, especially for mid-school entry at Class VIII‚ÄìIX.
  • 5
    SAI for international-minded families Parents at SAI specifically mentioned the IGCSE track as the deciding factor for their choice ‚Äî several had family plans for children to study abroad for undergraduate degrees, and CAIE familiarity gives a genuine advantage in international university applications.

Important Notes Before You Decide

üìã Before You Finalise Any School

  1. Verify directly with the school. Fees mentioned here are based on parent interviews, school websites, and aggregator platforms as of February 2026. Schools revise fees annually ‚Äî always get the official 2026-27 fee circular from the admissions office before making a financial commitment.
  2. Budget 15‚Äì25% extra. Most private schools charge separately for transport, uniforms, books, lab fees, sports and extracurricular activities. The figures above reflect core tuition and school fees only.
  3. KV admissions timeline. Check the KVS admissions portal (kvsonlineadmission.kvs.gov.in) for seat availability and the exact admission calendar for 2026-27. Priority categories are strictly enforced ‚Äî the window opens early in the year and fills fast.
  4. Sainik School ‚Äî confirm current fees. GoI revises Sainik School fees periodically. Contact the school directly for the confirmed 2026-27 fee structure before planning your budget.
  5. AISSEE for Sainik School. The All India Sainik Schools Entrance Examination is competitive and has a specific preparation path ‚Äî start at least 6‚Äì8 months before the exam date if your child is interested.
