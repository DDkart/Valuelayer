Broker Commission: One Month's Rent, Gone Before You Unpack

This is the single most common shock for people moving to Bhubaneswar from smaller towns or directly from college. The broker fee is not part of the rent. It is not adjustable into the deposit. It goes entirely to the broker. And here is the part that stings most: the broker often does nothing more than show you three flats, call the landlord, and stand in the room while you both sign the agreement.

üí° What you can do: Platforms like NoBroker and OLX have direct landlord listings in Bhubaneswar, particularly in Patia and Kalarahanga. If you do use a broker, negotiate: brokers near Patia Chowk and Damana Square often accept half a month's rent for budget flats under ‚Çπ10,000/month.

‚Çπ18,000 ‚Äì ‚Çπ60,000 ¬∑ refundable but locked

The standard security deposit across Bhubaneswar is 2 months' rent. On a ‚Çπ10,000/month flat, that is ‚Çπ20,000. On a ‚Çπ18,000 2BHK near Nandan Vihar in CSP, that is ‚Çπ36,000. Buildings with fully furnished fittings ‚Äî AC, geyser, washing machine, beds ‚Äî sometimes ask for 3 months citing the replacement cost of appliances. üí° What you can do: Clarify before paying: "Yeh token kahan adjust hoga ‚Äî deposit mein ya pahele mahine ke rent mein?" Get the answer in writing on WhatsApp at minimum, so there is a timestamp. Never hand over cash without at least a photograph of the landlord's written confirmation.

‚Çπ200‚Äì‚Çπ800/month normally ¬∑ ‚Çπ1,000‚Äì‚Çπ2,000/trip in summer

Municipal water in Bhubaneswar is managed by the Bhubaneswar Municipal Corporation (BMC). In apartments and gated societies, water charges are bundled into maintenance. In independent buildings across most Patia lanes, landlords charge for water separately ‚Äî typically ‚Çπ200‚Äì‚Çπ800/month.

Setup Costs Inside the Flat

"Furnished" Rarely Means What You Think It Means

‚Çπ3,000 ‚Äì ‚Çπ8,000 ¬∑ first-week shopping run

In Bhubaneswar, a flat advertised as "furnished" typically includes: beds with mattresses, a wardrobe, a dining table with chairs, fans, a geyser, and sometimes an AC. It almost never includes: kitchen utensils, bedsheets, pillow covers, a washing machine (unless specifically stated), curtains, a mop and broom, a bucket and mug, or cookware. A "semi-furnished" flat typically means only the basic fixtures ‚Äî fans, lights, sometimes a geyser. No furniture at all.

The first-week shopping run after moving in ‚Äî kitchen basics, bedsheets, basic cleaning supplies, a dustbin, hangers, small shelves ‚Äî typically costs ‚Çπ3,000‚Äì‚Çπ8,000 depending on how much you bring from home.

üí° Where to buy cheaply: The Unit-1 Haat and shops along Saheed Nagar Main Road are the cheapest places to buy household basics in Bhubaneswar. Avoid the shops immediately around KIIT Square where prices for the same items run 20‚Äì30% higher because of the student crowd. For bedsheets and kitchen items, the Sunday market at Baramunda Bus Stand is worth a trip.

üí° What you can do: Always ask: "Rent ke upar maintenance alag se lagta hai kya, aur kitna?" And separately: "Society ka ek-baar ka deposit bhi hai kya?" Get both numbers before you calculate whether you can afford the flat.

Summer Power Backup: The Surcharge That Appears in April

‚Çπ500‚Äì‚Çπ1,500/month ¬∑ April to June only

Bhubaneswar's April‚ÄìJune summer involves regular load-shedding, especially in non-BDA colonies and independent buildings. Buildings with DG generator sets run the backup at a cost ‚Äî and that cost is passed to tenants, usually as a monthly surcharge on the electricity bill. In practice this is ‚Çπ500‚Äì‚Çπ1,500/month during peak summer months. This cost does not appear anywhere in your rental agreement.

üí° What you can do: Ask the landlord directly: "Bijli cut hone pe DG ka charge alag se lagta hai?" And ask the security guard or existing tenants how many hours of backup they actually get per day and whether it covers ACs ‚Äî because many buildings run backup for lights and fans only.

‚Çπ300‚Äì‚Çπ800/month bike ¬∑ ‚Çπ800‚Äì‚Çπ1,500/month car

In older independent buildings across Patia and Raghunathpur, street parking is free and largely informal. In gated societies near Niladri Vihar and CSP, parking is frequently a paid add-on. Some societies allocate only one parking slot per flat and charge extra for a second vehicle. This is rarely mentioned during the flat showing.

üí° What you can do: If you have a vehicle, confirm parking terms before signing. Ask specifically: "Ek bike ke liye parking ka kharcha kitna hai aur kahan milega?" Two people I know spent months arguing with society committees about parking that was never clarified upfront.

‚Çπ200‚Äì‚Çπ500 facilitation ¬∑ one morning

In Odisha, landlords are legally required to get new tenants verified by the local police ‚Äî submitting your Aadhaar, PAN, and rental agreement to the nearest police station. For Patia residents this is the Patia Police Station on Nandankanan Road. For CSP residents, the Chandrasekharpur Police Station near CSP P.S. Square handles it.
