üìã 5 Things to Always Do Before Leaving Your Laptop

  1. Get a written or WhatsApp quote before authorizing any work ‚Äî amount, parts, and labour broken out separately.
  2. Ask what type of screen panel they're fitting ‚Äî OEM original, OEM-grade compatible, or generic. These are different things at different price points.
  3. Ask whether the quote includes labour and GST. What sounds like ‚Çπ6,000 can become ‚Çπ7,200 after these are added if you don't confirm upfront.
  4. Ask about the warranty on parts and labour. Good third-party shops typically give 1‚Äì3 months. Authorized centres give 3‚Äì6 months on parts. Get this in writing or WhatsApp.
  5. For HP Authorized: Ask to see the screen panel in sealed HP-original packaging before installation. Multiple customers have raised authenticity concerns publicly ‚Äî you are entitled to ask.
