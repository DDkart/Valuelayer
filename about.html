About ValueLayer

Last updated: February 2026

ValueLayer is Bhubaneswar's independent cost-of-living guide — built by locals, for anyone who calls the Temple City home or is thinking about it.

We started with a simple frustration: rent listings, grocery prices, school fees, and transport costs in Bhubaneswar were scattered across WhatsApp groups, outdated blog posts, and hearsay. There was no single, honest, ground-level resource that told you what life here actually costs in rupees, not estimates from Delhi-based websites.

So we decided to build one.

What We Do

Every price you see on ValueLayer is field-verified. Our team visits Unit-1 Haat, Damana Market, Saheed Nagar, and local neighbourhoods regularly to track real prices — not averages taken from national databases.

We cover:

  • Rental markets across every major area of Bhubaneswar
  • Grocery and food costs — from street stalls to supermarkets
  • School fees, coaching costs, and education expenses
  • Transport and commute costs for different income levels
  • Healthcare, utilities, and everyday services
  • Lifestyle guides — weekend plans, dining, and local culture

Our Promise

No sponsored rankings. No affiliate-inflated numbers. No copy-pasted data from national portals. Every article on ValueLayer is written with the intention of being genuinely useful to someone standing at a crossroads — whether that's a fresh graduate taking their first job, a family deciding which neighbourhood to settle in, or someone returning to Odisha after years away.

Bhubaneswar is growing fast. We're here to help you navigate it.

The Team

We're a small team of Bhubaneswar residents — writers, researchers, and obsessive note-takers who genuinely enjoy wandering the city's markets, talking to auto drivers, and asking vendors uncomfortable questions about pricing. If you've seen someone scribbling in a notebook near Unit-1 Haat, that was probably us.
